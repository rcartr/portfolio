About Me

My name is Richard Carter.

I am originally from the great state of Georgia, although I grew up mostly in Tennessee. Currently I live near Dallas, TX.

I am an Army veteran, serving a total of 8 years between active duty service and active National Guard service, with multiple overseas deployments. I'm very proud of my service and have carried so many lessons learned during my service into my regular civilian life. I am also a father, raising my three children who all live with me and range from 8-14 years of age.

My goal is to become a web/software developer. I have an adjacent interest in crypto technologies and would like to eventually move into that industry as I learn more and improve as a developer. In the meantime, I want to learn as much as I can and contribute growth to great companies, regardless of their size. I am an intelligent and motivated worker; I am dedicated to my family and providing for them and look forward to experiencing this beautiful life with them.
